# How to Reset Your Password

## Overview

This section will guide you through resetting your password and regaining access to your account.

## When to Use This Guide

Use these steps if you cannot log in and need to reset your password.

## How to Reset Your Password

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Check your email for the password reset message.
6. Click the reset link in the email.
7. Enter and confirm your new password.

## Troubleshooting

### Reset link expired

Password reset links expire after a limited time for security reasons.
If your link has expired, request a new password reset email and use the newest link.

### Reset email not received

Check your spam or junk folder.
If the email still does not appear, verify that you entered the correct email address.

### Reset link already used

Password reset links can only be used once.
Request a new reset email if the link has already been used.

## Still Need Help?

If you are still unable to reset your password, contact support and include:

* the email address used for your account
* the time you attempted the reset
